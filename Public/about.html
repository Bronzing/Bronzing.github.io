About Me

Experience

For the last 14 years I have been a stay at home parent for my 3
children and have now decided to embark on a new chapter, gaining
skills that I find challenging and engaging.

While I am new to the world of coding and web development,
I am currently looking for opportunities to help others while also
testing and strengthening my current capabilities.

Hobbies and Interests

I enjoy cooking from cultures all around the world and
experimenting in the kitchen, using my family as willing
guinea pigs.

When I find the time I also love art in the form of sketching
from still life, and more recently with print making.

Sewing and wood-making are two other hobbies that I've
dipped my toes in, and am finding great satisfaction in learning.

Coding is my newest passion, I have found a commonality
between all these interests; at the completion of anyone of them
there exists something that hadn't before. This creation of new
useful food, art, clothes, furniture, and tools is something I find
deeply satisfying and fulfilling.
