Best Movies According to Bethelynn

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Princess Bride

From the comedy, to the love story, this story is timeless and well done.

Song of the Sea

A touching story about two young siblings, set with irish myths and lore.
They have to come to terms with loss and learn to rely on each other.
Heart-wrenching and warming, with beautiful animations.

In Brudges

A gritty drama and dark comedy, that takes unusual twists and turns throughout.
Well acted and written, it's a fun if sometimes uncomfortable watch.
